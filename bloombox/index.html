ABOUT US

Bloombox is a dynamic and innovative platform dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship among students. It serves as a nurturing ground for aspiring entrepreneurs, providing them with the resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities needed to transform their ideas into successful ventures. The cell organizes various workshops, seminars, and competitions to cultivate entrepreneurial skills and encourage creative thinking. By connecting students with industry experts and successful entrepreneurs, Bloombox aims to create a vibrant entrepreneurial ecosystem within the college, empowering students to become the business leaders of tomorrow.

OUR LATEST EVENT

E-SUMMIT 2024

E-Summit 2024, organized by Bloombox, was a resounding success and a significant milestone for the college's entrepreneurial community. The event brought together a diverse group of aspiring entrepreneurs, industry leaders, and business professionals for an inspiring and informative experience. Highlights of E-Summit 2024 included keynote speeches from renowned industry experts who shared their journeys and insights into the world of entrepreneurship. Panel discussions delved into current trends and challenges in various industries, providing valuable perspectives and actionable advice.

Participants had the opportunity to engage in hands-on workshops, gaining practical skills in areas such as business planning, marketing, and financial management. The competitive pitching sessions were a standout feature, where budding entrepreneurs presented their innovative ideas to a panel of judges, receiving constructive feedback and the chance to win seed funding. The networking sessions facilitated meaningful connections between students, mentors, and potential investors, fostering an environment of collaboration and support. E-Summit 2024 truly embodied the spirit of innovation and creativity that Bloombox aims to cultivate, leaving a lasting impact on all who attended and inspiring the next wave of entrepreneurial leaders.
